About me

Fascination with art and design, from classical to modern, has always been a part of who I am. I love to mix design and technology to create things and solve problems through design. My passion for problem-solving and interest in technology has drawn me to the world of digital design. With these in mind, I found UX/UI design as a skill that covers my interests and my abilities. My goal is creating effective and intuitive interfaces design, I like prototyping, it helps me think through interaction models and new possibilities. I have always been passionate about learning new skills and i like working in a team. Painting, travelling, watching movies, cooking,and reading are all parts of my life that allow me to be myself and enjoy life.
